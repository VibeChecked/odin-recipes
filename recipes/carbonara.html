Authentic Carbonara Recipe

Description: 15 min recipe for true Italiana Carbonara

Here are 5 (technically 6) ingredients needed

  • Pasta, 1 serving
  • Guanciale, 4 ounces diced
  • Egg Yolks, 4 pax
  • Pecorino,Romano, 3/4 cup approx
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt

No olive oil, butter, cream or god forbid garlic. Because italiano

  1. Start by bringing a large pot of water to a boil for your pasta (donâ€™t forget to salt your water!). Place a dry skillet over medium heat and add 4 ounces of diced guanciale.
  2. When water boils and the guanciale starts to sizzle, grab a large glass bowl and whisk 4 egg yolks, 3/4 cup of finely shredded pecorino romano and a big pinch of freshly cracked black pepper until it is thick and creamy.
  3. As soon as the guanciale is crisp, take it off of the heat and let it relax until the pasta is cooked.
  4. Transfer the pasta. It's okay for some water to be transferred over, but do not throw out the pasta water just yet
  5. Toss the pasta in the sauce and then add the crisp guanciale and maybe a little extra pecorino romano and toss to combine. Add pasta water till satisfication

Addtional notes

Egg yolks preferred. Whites tend to "stiffen" the sauce up if not done in perfect timing. Whites cogulate easily, better to leave it out for another dish.
